Billie Bugara

Billie Bugara is a 24-year-old research student and media consultant. She is a masters candidate at New York University in the school's Media, Culture, and Communication program. Jump to Section Her research is situated in disciplines of visual culture, semiotics, and history. Her interests mainly center around the aesthetics of consumer culture, material culture, and technology.

She has been interviewed by outlets such as Insider, Pitchfork, Complex, and more for her insight on modern digital culture, while also contributing pieces of her own to i-D, Complex, and more. 1 She has worked with companies such as SoundCloud, Atlantic Records, and Year0001 Records as a media specialist in various roles.

Her hobbyist art (music, design, etc.), can be found via her project Spring on the Avenue.

Early Life

Billie Bugara was born on September 17, 2000 in Alliance, Ohio. She attended Marlington High School, graduating in 2019. She was vice presidente of Spanish National Honor Society among other things.

Education

Boston University

Upon graduating high school, Bugara moved to Boston, Massachusetts to attend Boston University. She graduated in 2023 with a Bachelors of Science in Media Science, a Bachelors of Arts in Sociology, and a minor in Journalism.

New York University

After graduating from BU, Bugara moved to New York City to attend New York University. She is currently enrolled in the school's Media, Culture, and Communication program as a masters candidate.

Career

Journalism

Beginning in high school and throughout her time in Boston, Bugara worked as a freelance music journalist. She has written for publications like i-D, Complex, Bandcamp Daily. She was also an editorial writer for Lyrical Lemonade from 2020-2022.

SoundCloud

Coinciding with her journalism work was her role at music streaming platform SoundCloud. She worked here from 2020-2022, providing editorial playlist curation2, social media management, and other miscellaneous roles.

Personal Life
